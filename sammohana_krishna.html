Sammohana Krishnar Temple, Mohanur

The Sammohana Krishnar Temple is located in Mohanur, Namakkal district, Tamil Nadu. This unique temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna, known here as Sammohana Krishnar, who is believed to possess the power to attract and enchant devotees. The temple is situated on the banks of the sacred river Cauvery and is renowned for its serene atmosphere and spiritual significance.

Devotees visit this temple to seek blessings for harmony, peace, and the removal of obstacles. The temple is especially popular during Krishna Jayanthi and other important festivals. The Sammohana Krishna Stotram is recited here for spiritual upliftment and fulfillment of wishes.

Location: Mohanur, Namakkal District, Tamil Nadu, India
Deity: Sammohana Krishnar (Lord Krishna)
